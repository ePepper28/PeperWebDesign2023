Veterinarian

Description

Veterinarians treat the injuries and illnesses of pets and other animals with a variety of medical equipment, including surgical tools and x-ray and ultrasound machines.

Average Salary

80k-180k is the average salary annually. It may vary depending if you have your own practice or if you specify in a specific profession that costs more money.

Employers

  • Private Clinics
  • Corporate Animal Health and Care
  • Veterinary Hospitals
  • Priate Practices
  • Farms
  • Zoos

Related Careers

  • Zoologists and Zookeepers
    •  Study zoo animal life, take care of the animals, and you are located at a zoo.
  • Wildlife Specialist and Park Rangers
    • Specializes in taking care of the animals in your specified area, making sure people are not interfering, studying of the animals, and lots of watching and studying.

Detailed Information

Diagnose, treat, or research diseases and injuries of animals. Includes veterinarians who conduct research and development, inspect livestock, or care for pets and companion animals.
