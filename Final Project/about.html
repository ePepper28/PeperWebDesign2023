Life Before Fame

On February 16, 1990, Abel Makkonen Tesfaye was born in Ontario, Canada. When he was just a toddler, his parents seperated leaving just him and his mom. They both lived in the tough Scarborough neighborhood of Toronto. As a child, Abel was very introverted and shy. When he was 11th grade, he dropped out of school and left home, devoting his life to full time partying. Eventually, he ended up landing a job at American Apparel, and began writing music. He uploaded some of his first songs on Youtube in 2011. A few of his videos were major hits and gained the attention of producers. He then signed a record label, and he founded XO Labels.

Music

The Weeknd is currently the most popular music artist in the world! With 57,653,305,361 streams total, he has won his fans with his mix of pop, and R&B music. His first ever song to go completely viral was "Wicked Games" in 2013 which made its way to the Top 100 Songs Billboard. Since then, he has 183 released songs as a lead and as a featuring artist.

Net Worth

The Weeknd is currently worth $300 million! He has earned most of his money from millions of his albums and singles selling worldwide. His songs are on every single music platform which get billions of streams each. He also has gone on 7 individual tours in his career which were extremely profitable.
