The Weeknd

Abel Tasfaye, famously known as The Weeknd, is an extremely talented musical artist. Through his talent, he created a widely known name for himself and has been rewarded for his abilities. He has been rewarded for his accomplishments through Grammies, American Music Awards, Billboard Music Awards, People's Choice Awards, and hundreds of other forms of awards. Total, he has won 120 awards, in counting, in his career.
